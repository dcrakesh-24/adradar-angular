## Why Integrate LinkedIn with Recotap?

Connecting your LinkedIn to AdRadar allows you to:

- **Identify Account-Level Engagement**: Reveal exactly which companies are viewing and clicking your ads, beyond basic LinkedIn metrics.
- **Track Revenue Attribution**: Tie LinkedIn ad spend directly to CRM opportunities and closed-won revenue to prove ROI.
- **Automate Journey Mapping**: Categorize accounts into stages like Awareness or Intent based on their actual ad interactions.
- **Optimize Budget Distribution**: Apply impression capping to ensure ads reach your entire target list instead of a few accounts.
- **Maximize ROI with Campaign Scheduling**: Run ads during peak activity windows to reach your audience when they are most active.
- **Refine Reach with Auto-Blocking**: Automatically exclude irrelevant companies to ensure your ads only reach the right accounts.

## How to Connect Your LinkedIn Ad Account With Recotap

1. Login to your AdRadar account and navigate to **Integrations** on the left-hand side.
2. Click **Connect** on the LinkedIn dialogue box.
3. You will be redirected to LinkedIn’s login page.
4. Enter your credentials and select **Log In**.
5. Click **Accept** to authorize access.
6. Configure the Ad Account:
   - You will be taken to the **Configure LinkedIn** page in AdRadar.
   - Select your associated LinkedIn Campaign Manager ad account from the dropdown where you wish to run your campaigns.
   - Click **Save** to finalize the setup.
7. Integration is complete. Your LinkedIn account is now connected to AdRadar and ready for campaign monitoring and optimization.
