## What is AdRadar?

AdRadar is a LinkedIn-focused ABM intelligence and optimization platform built by Recotap. It helps B2B marketing teams
monitor, analyze, and optimize LinkedIn advertising campaigns at the **account level** instead of just ad or campaign level.

Unlike LinkedIn Campaign Manager, which primarily shows **ad-level metrics**, AdRadar provides **company-level insights** so
marketers can see which accounts are engaging, influencing the pipeline, and driving revenue.

AdRadar connects **LinkedIn Ads, CRM data, and campaign engagement signals** to provide a unified view of performance. It
helps marketing teams prove the **true ROI of LinkedIn advertising** and optimize spend on the accounts that matter most.

## Key capabilities of AdRadar

Key capabilities of AdRadar include:

- Account-level LinkedIn ads analytics
- Campaign performance monitoring for ABM programs
- Pipeline and revenue influence tracking
- Impression capping to prevent budget waste
- Audience exclusion and targeting optimization
- Campaign scheduling based on buyer activity
- Buyer journey stage mapping for target accounts
- CRM integration for opportunity and revenue tracking

In simple terms, AdRadar transforms LinkedIn Ads from a campaign reporting tool into a full ABM intelligence system.
