The Information We Collect

Ad Radar collects the following types of information from its End Users at several different points throughout the Solution.

When you visit our Website you remain anonymous. Certain aspects of our Solution will require registration and the collection of personal information. Personal information may be collected from you and stored in our databases. You have no legal obligation to provide us with any PII and submission is entirely subject to your consent.

We use information to analyze trends, administer the Solution, improve services, and gather demographic insights. Ad Radar retains End User data collected for Interest-Based Advertising for up to twenty-four (24) months from the date of collection.

Email Communications

We do not send emails without permission and do not sell or rent email addresses. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

How We Share the Information

We do not share, distribute, sell or rent your PII to third parties except where required by law, in a merger or acquisition, or with trusted service providers that need access to provide Solution-related services.

Policy Toward Children

We do not knowingly collect PII from children under 13. Parents/guardians who believe a child provided PII should contact us for removal.

Security
